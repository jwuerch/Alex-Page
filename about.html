Law Offices of
Alex J. Higgins

Labor and Employment Law

Alex has successfully represented both employees and employers in various settings. He was a partner in the law firm of Stokes Lawrence, until January 2004.

Alex graduated from the University of Washington School of Law in 1991, and then served as a law clerk in U.S. District Court.

Alex has also worked as a U.S. Park Ranger in Montana and a high school history teacher in Mexico.

Strength and Experience




Alex is an experienced employment attorney, having tried numerous cases in both state and federal court. He is currently a member of the Executive Committee of the Washington State Bar Association, Labor and Employment Section.
